The story behind Krutrim AI

Most AI models speak English first. Krutrim set out to build one that speaks our language — literally. Born out of the belief that India's 1.4 billion people deserve AI that understands Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, and 17 more languages as naturally as it understands English.

Today, Krutrim powers everything from customer support chatbots in Bengaluru to farm advisory systems in rural Maharashtra.
